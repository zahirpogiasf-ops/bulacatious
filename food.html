Food and Delicacies

Taste the famous flavors of Bulacan.

Pastillas de Leche

Soft milk candies made from sweet carabao’s milk and wrapped in colorful paper designs.

Where to try: San Miguel, Bulacan

Inipit

A sweet dessert made of chiffon cake layers with creamy custard filling.

Where to try: Local bakeries in Bulacan

Empanada de Kaliskis

A flaky savory pastry filled with chicken, potatoes, and flavorful spices.

Where to try: Malolos, Bulacan

Chicharon

Crispy pork rinds famous in Santa Maria, Bulacan and loved nationwide.

Where to try: Santa Maria, Bulacan
