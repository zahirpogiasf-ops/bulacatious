Festivals and Culture

Experience colorful celebrations and traditions.

Singkaban Festival

Location: Malolos City, Bulacan

Month Celebrated: September 8–15

The Singkaban Festival celebrates Bulacan’s culture, arts, and history through colorful street dances, bamboo arch decorations, music, and traditional Filipino performances.

Obando Fertility Rites

Location: Obando, Bulacan

Month Celebrated: May 17–19

The Obando Fertility Rites is a famous cultural and religious festival where devotees dance in the streets while praying for fertility, blessings, and good fortune.
