Travel Tips

Helpful reminders before visiting Bulacan.

  • Best time to visit Bulacan is during the dry season from November to May.
  • Public buses and private vehicles are the easiest transportation options from Metro Manila.
  • Bring extra clothes and water when visiting outdoor attractions and adventure sites.
  • Always keep your belongings secure and follow local safety guidelines.
  • Respect local traditions, churches, and cultural practices during festivals and events.
